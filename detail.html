Description

With an ideal location just five minutes from Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) and 10 minutes from Cyber City, the Radisson Blu Plaza Delhi Airport provides upscale accommodations and convenient city access. Situated on the Delhi-Gurgaon Expressway, our hotel places guests near New Delhi’s vibrant South and Central sides and close to the bustling commercial hub of Gurugram (formerly Gurgaon). When you’re ready to explore the area, head to the nearest metro station, only 600 meters away, or enjoy complimentary transportation to world-class shopping at nearby Aerocity. After business meetings or area excursions, return to the hotel for a relaxing massage at our award-winning day spa, R-The Spa. Set on three levels, the extensive wellness and beauty facility includes an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a yoga room. Great food and drinks await you at our on-site restaurants and bars. Planning your next adventure? Let our knowledgeable concierge recommend the best places to visit.
